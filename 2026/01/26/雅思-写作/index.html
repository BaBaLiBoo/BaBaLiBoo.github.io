雅思写作

📕笔记

  • Task1-小作文

    • 图表作文
    • 150+字数(200)
    • 20min
    • 1/3分值

  • Task2-大作文

    • 250+words
    • 40min

一、小作文

1、Pie Charts

4段

  • ① introduction

    同义词替换 + 扩展图表标题

  • ② overview (2 sentences, big similariities+differences)

    Overall, 什么增了、什么减了、什么没变

  • ③④ 分组 details

    比较对象A+B / 增长指标+减少指标

    补充：xxx become the most widely (spoken) …

2、Line Graph

① Summery

  • framework
    • The (line) graph demonstrates the …(main motivation) in 1990,2000 and 2010
  • trend
    • The years witnessed (a large number of) …

② Groups

  • why grouping
    • According to the graph, the major reason for A was ….
    • It was a different story for B and C: both had the same number at the start of the period, much lower than that of A.
  • comparison and key features

💬 小作文常用表达

  • 引入：

    Overall

    By contrast，By comparison

    It’s a different story for，It’s noticeable that

  • 连词：

    转折：whereas

    继续：Subsequently，Moreover，Additionally，Likewise

  • 时间

    十年：decade, ten-year period

    over the period

  • 变化：

    增：rise，see increases，grow，more prevalent

    减：fall, decrease, decline，downward trend(be observed)

    减半：be halved，halving from 20% to 10%

    go up/down by 变化幅度 to 数值

    超：more than, exceed

    保持：remain unchanged/stable

    低于：stay below

    峰值：peaked at

    turning points, intersection(交汇点)

  • 比例：

    proportion，percentage, share

    占比：account for, make up，represent

    总计：of the total

    second-largest

    dominate

    spending，expenditure，costs

  • 关系：

    相等：same，equal，equivalent figure，counterpart

    相比较：relatively（small），comparatively（lower）

  • 形容：

    steeply，sharply

    consistently

Overall, the share proportion of students who spoke only English or only French declined over the decade only speak English or only have French as their other language has fallen over the decade~~~~., while Spanish and multilingual ability became more prevalent.Besides, students whose additional language was only Spanish and who had two other languages accounted for a larger share in 2010 than ten years ago~~~~,whereas **In contrast, the proportion of students who spoke only German remained unchanged.**the percentage of students who only spoke German besides English kept the same.

Those able to speak two other languages also increased, from 10% to 15% over the same period. Furthermore, both of the proportions of “Spanish only” and “Another language” has gone up by 5% in the decade, to 35% and 20% relatively.Additionaly, from 2000 to 2010, students who had two other languages also rose from 10% to 15%.
